About

I'm Lex Parkinson, a Creative Director with a background in design, video production, and photography. Over the past decade, I’ve led visual projects across proptech, insurance, and agency environments—bringing ideas to life through thoughtful creative and strong execution.

 

My work bridges strategy and storytelling. Whether I’m directing a brand film, designing a campaign, or shooting on location, I aim to make things that are clear, engaging, and built to last. I’ve developed content for commercial clients, internal teams, and everything in between—always with an eye on quality and purpose.

 

I’m currently open to new opportunities—whether full-time, part-time or freelance—and always interested in collaborating on something meaningful. Let’s talk.
